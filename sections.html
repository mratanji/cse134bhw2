Articles should have a heading

Articles are meant to be independent sections of pages that are redistributable. Typically, this means that they are blog posts or comments or other things that can be moved page to page. Here, it is just this simple text block, but they can also follow schemas to help with formatting.

Sections should also have a heading

Sections are meant to just split areas of pages up, they don't do much otherwise. Unless you are building an outline or something, you dont need to use sections. It's reccomended to use <div> instead.
